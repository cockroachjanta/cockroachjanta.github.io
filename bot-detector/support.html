Support

Common questions

  • No badges appear on comments. Make sure the extension toggle (in the popup) is on, then refresh the YouTube tab. Badges appear as comments load while you scroll.
  • Ages don't show, only "bot vibes" flags. Account-age detection needs a free YouTube Data API key. Open the popup, follow the "get a free key" link, paste the key, and hit "lock it in".
  • The banner says a section is organic but I see spam. The extension measures account age, not comment content — an old account posting spam won't be flagged. Tighten or loosen the threshold in the popup.
  • How do I delete everything it saved? Popup → gear icon → "wipe receipts". All local data is removed permanently.

Report a problem

Reach the team through the Cockroach Janta Party website, or use the publisher contact listed on the extension's Chrome Web Store page. Include:

  • what you expected and what happened instead,
  • the video URL where it happened, and
  • your Chrome version (chrome://version).

Privacy

Wondering what data the extension keeps? Short answer: everything stays on your device. Full details in the privacy policy.
