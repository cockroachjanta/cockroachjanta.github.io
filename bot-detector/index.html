CJP Bot Detector

A Chrome extension that catches bot comments on YouTube — by checking how old the commenting accounts actually are. no cap.

What it does

  • Scans comments as you scroll on any YouTube video.
  • Flags comments from suspiciously new accounts with a 🚩 sus af badge, and shows every commenter's account age (🪳 +3 years old).
  • Pins a live verdict above the comments: 🚨 it's giving bot farm when bot density is high, or ✅ comments passed the vibe check when things look organic.
  • Keeps the receipts — a local database of every account it identifies (creation date, channel name, videos where it commented), exportable as JSON.

See it work

Private by design

Everything stays on your device. No servers, no tracking, no data collection. To read a commenter's public account age, the extension asks YouTube directly — the same public data the site itself shows — without your login. Prefer the official API? Add a free key in the popup.
